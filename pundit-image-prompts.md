# Pundit Avatar Image Prompts

Use these prompts to generate **1:1 square portraits** that can be cropped into circles in the sidebar.

## Global Style Prompt (prepend to each)

Create a stylized digital portrait for a football analysis app avatar.  
Framing: head and shoulders, centered, looking at camera, neutral dark gradient background.  
Lighting: dramatic studio rim light, high contrast, sharp facial detail.  
Style: editorial sports illustration, modern, slightly cinematic, not photoreal clone of any real person.  
Mood: confident pundit energy.  
Output: 1024x1024 PNG, no text, no watermark, no logos, no badge, no background clutter.

## 1) The Analyst

**Prompt**

The Analyst: composed, intelligent football data expert in their 30s-40s, subtle glasses, neat blazer over dark knit, calm expression, analytical gaze, slight teal accent lighting, feels precise and numbers-driven.

## 2) The Coach

**Prompt**

The Coach: experienced tactical manager type in their 40s-50s, training jacket with minimal details, focused serious expression, slightly furrowed brow, hands just visible near chest as if explaining shape and movement, strong sideline authority.

## 3) The Fan

**Prompt**

The Fan: passionate die-hard supporter in their 20s-40s, club-colored scarf (generic color blocks only, no official logos), animated hopeful expression, energetic posture, emotional but lovable intensity, matchday atmosphere in lighting.

## 4) The Banter Merchant

**Prompt**

The Banter Merchant: witty pub-football character in their 30s-50s, cheeky grin, raised eyebrow, casual jacket/hoodie combo, playful confidence, lively social energy, feels like someone about to drop a bold hot take.

## 5) The Skeptic

**Prompt**

The Skeptic: sharp, measured contrarian in their 30s-50s, minimalist dark outfit, thoughtful side-glance, slightly unimpressed expression, understated confidence, tone of someone challenging hype with realism.

## Optional Negative Prompt

blurry, low resolution, extra limbs, deformed face, text, watermark, logo, football club crests, copyright characters, celebrity likeness, cluttered background, meme style, cartoon exaggeration
